Bengaluru Travel Guide:

General Information:
- Bengaluru is known as the Silicon Valley of India.
- Famous for IT hubs, pleasant weather, and gardens.
- Main language: Kannada, English, Hindi.

Accommodation:
- Average hotel cost per night: ₹3500
- Budget hotels: ₹1200 to ₹2000 per night
- Luxury hotels: ₹7000 and above per night

Transportation:
- Metro (Namma Metro) is available in major areas.
- Autos and buses are common.
- Average daily transport cost: ₹180
- App-based cabs like Ola and Uber are widely used.

Food:
- Average meal cost: ₹250
- Famous local foods: Masala Dosa, Bisi Bele Bath, Filter Coffee
- Popular food streets: VV Puram Food Street, Koramangala

Popular Attractions:
- Lalbagh Botanical Garden
- Cubbon Park
- Bangalore Palace
- ISKCON Temple
- Bannerghatta National Park
- Nandi Hills (nearby)

Recommended Stay Duration:
- 3 to 5 days is ideal for Bengaluru.
