Mumbai Travel Guide:

General Information:
- Mumbai is the financial capital of India.
- Best time to visit: October to February.
- Main language: Marathi, Hindi, and English.

Accommodation:
- Average hotel cost per night: ₹4000
- Budget hotels: ₹1500 to ₹2500 per night
- Luxury hotels: ₹8000 and above per night

Transportation:
- Mumbai local train is the fastest way to travel.
- Metro and BEST buses are widely available.
- Average daily transport cost: ₹200
- Auto-rickshaws are not allowed in South Mumbai.

Food:
- Average meal cost: ₹300
- Famous local foods: Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Misal Pav, Seafood
- Popular food areas: Juhu Beach, Mohammed Ali Road, Colaba Causeway

Popular Attractions:
- Gateway of India
- Marine Drive
- Elephanta Caves
- Juhu Beach
- Siddhivinayak Temple
- Bandra-Worli Sea Link

Recommended Stay Duration:
- 2 to 4 days is ideal for exploring Mumbai.
